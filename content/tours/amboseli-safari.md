Amboseli National Park is famous for being the best place in Africa to get close to free-ranging elephants among other wildlife species. Other attractions of the park include opportunities to meet the Masai people and also visit the Masai village.

### Itinerary Highlights
- **Day 1**: Arrival and evening game drive at the foot of Kilimanjaro
- **Day 2-3**: Full day game drives tracking the big tuskers
- **Day 4**: Morning views and departure
