Tsavo is one of the oldest and largest parks in Kenya. Split into East and West, it offers massive herds of dust-red elephants, rhino sanctuaries, and dramatic volcanic landscapes.

### Itinerary Highlights
- **Day 1**: Train to Tsavo West, evening game drive
- **Day 2**: Mzima Springs and rhino sanctuary
- **Day 3**: Transfer to Tsavo East
- **Day 4**: Full day tracking large elephant herds
- **Day 5**: Morning game drive and return
