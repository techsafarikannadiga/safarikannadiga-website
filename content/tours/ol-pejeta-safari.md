Ol Pejeta Conservancy is a pioneer in conservation, home to the last two northern white rhinos on the planet and the only place in Kenya to see chimpanzees. It offers a unique blend of wildlife viewing and conservation education.

### Itinerary Highlights
- **Day 1**: Drive to Nanyuki, evening game drive
- **Day 2**: Visit the Rhino cemetery and Chimpanzee sanctuary
- **Day 3**: Morning lion tracking and departure
