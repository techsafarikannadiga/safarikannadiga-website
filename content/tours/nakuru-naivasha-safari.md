A perfect combination of bird life and big game. Lake Nakuru is famous for its rhinos and flamingos, while Lake Naivasha offers unique boat safaris and walking tours among giraffes and zebras.

### Itinerary Highlights
- **Day 1**: Drive to Lake Nakuru, afternoon game drive
- **Day 2**: Full day in Nakuru Rhino Sanctuary
- **Day 3**: Transfer to Naivasha, boat ride and walking safari
- **Day 4**: Hell's Gate National Park visit and return
