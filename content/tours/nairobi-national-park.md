The only incredible wildlife park located within a capital city. Nairobi National Park offers the surreal experience of seeing lions, rhinos, and giraffes against the backdrop of modern skyscrapers.

### Itinerary Highlights
- **Morning**: 6:00 AM pickup for optimal lighting and predator activity
- **Mid-morning**: Visit the Ivory Burning Site monument
- **Afternoon**: Optional visit to the giraffe center or elephant orphanage
