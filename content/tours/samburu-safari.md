Samburu National Reserve is a rugged and semi-desert park in Northern Kenya. It is home to the 'Special Five': Beisa Oryx, Reticulated Giraffe, Grevy's Zebra, Gerenuk, and Somali Ostrich.

### Itinerary Highlights
- **Day 1**: Flight to Samburu and transfer to camp
- **Day 2-4**: Searching for the unique northern species and leopards
- **Day 5**: Cultural village visit and departure
